Myer's Briggs 16 personalitys

The myers brigs test is a personality quiz that cataorgarises it's participents by 4 diffrent scalse

Extraversion (E) – Introversion (I)
 Do you find your self energised from socialisng or isolation.

Sensing (S) – Intuition (N)
 Do you gather information through reality or patterns and impressions?

Thinking (T) – Feeling (F)
 Do you make decisions based on emotions or facts and objective data?

Judging (J) – Prospecting (P)
 Do you deal with the world by making firm plans and decisions or do you prefer flexibility?

A final trait is added to the end.
Assertive (-A) - Turbulent (-A)
 Described as how you respond to success and failure.
 Do you feel confident in yourself or eager for self improvement?
